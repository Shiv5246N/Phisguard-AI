# Sample Emails for Testing PhishGuard-AI++

## Sample 1: Phishing Email (High Risk)
From: security@paypal-verify.com
To: user@example.com
Subject: Urgent: Your Account Will Be Suspended

Dear Customer,

We have detected unusual activity on your account. Your account will be 
suspended within 24 hours if you don't verify your identity immediately.

Click here to verify: http://paypal-verify.com/secure

Thank you,
PayPal Security Team

---

## Sample 2: Phishing Email (Medium Risk)
From: noreply@amazon-account-update.com
To: customer@example.com
Subject: Action Required: Update Your Payment Method

Hello,

Your payment method on file has expired. Please update your payment 
information to avoid service interruption.

Update Now: https://amazon-account-update.com/payment

Best regards,
Amazon Customer Service

---

## Sample 3: Legitimate Email (Low Risk)
From: noreply@github.com
To: user@example.com
Subject: GitHub: Password reset requested

Hello,

We received a request to reset your password. If you made this request, 
please click the link below:

https://github.com/password_reset?token=abc123xyz

If you didn't request this, you can safely ignore this email.

Best regards,
GitHub Support

---

## Sample 4: Suspicious Email (High Urgency)
From: support@bank-security-alert.com
To: account-holder@example.com
Subject: IMMEDIATE ACTION REQUIRED - Account Locked

URGENT SECURITY ALERT

Your account has been locked due to suspicious activity. You must verify 
your identity within 2 hours or your account will be permanently closed.

Verify Now: http://bank-security-alert.com/verify

This is your FINAL WARNING.

Security Team

---

## Sample 5: Legitimate Newsletter (Very Low Risk)
From: newsletter@company.com
To: subscriber@example.com
Subject: Monthly Newsletter - January 2024

Hi there,

Welcome to our monthly newsletter! Here's what's new this month:

- New product launches
- Upcoming events
- Special offers

Read more: https://company.com/newsletter/january-2024

Thanks for subscribing!

The Team
